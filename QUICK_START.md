## Deploy Your Aquarium Analyzer in 5 Minutes (FREE)

### Step 1: Create a GitHub Account (if you don't have one)
1. Go to https://github.com/signup
2. Create a free account

### Step 2: Upload the Code to GitHub
1. Go to https://github.com/new
2. Name your repository: `aquarium-analyzer`
3. Click "Create repository"
4. On the next page, click "uploading an existing file"
5. Drag and drop ALL these files:
   - `app_enhanced.py`
   - `requirements.txt`
   - `README.md`
6. Click "Commit changes"

### Step 3: Deploy to Streamlit Cloud
1. Go to https://share.streamlit.io/
2. Click "Sign in with GitHub"
3. Click "New app"
4. Select your repository (`aquarium-analyzer`)
5. Set Main file path: `app_enhanced.py`
6. Click "Deploy!"
7. Wait 2-3 minutes for deployment

### Step 4: Access on Your Phone
1. Copy the URL provided (something like `https://your-app.streamlit.app`)
2. Open it on your phone's browser
3. Tap the share button and "Add to Home Screen" for app-like access

## Using the App

### Testing Your Water

1. **Run your test strips** according to the SJ Wave instructions:
   - 10-in-1: Dip for 2 seconds, wait 15 seconds
   - Ammonia: Dip 30 times for 30 seconds

2. **Take photos** of each strip next to its color chart:
   - Use natural daylight
   - Avoid shadows
   - Keep camera steady

3. **Upload to the app**:
   - Select your water type (Saltwater)
   - Enter specific gravity if you have it
   - Upload your strip photos

4. **Sample colors**:
   - For each parameter, use the color picker
   - Click on your phone's screen to sample the exact color of each pad
   - The app will match it to the chart

5. **Get your report**:
   - Click "Analyze Water Parameters"
   - Review recommendations
   - Take action if needed!
